## DELIVERY NOTES

**On silence:** This script has more silence than most speakers are comfortable with. That discomfort is the point. The audience fills silence with meaning. Let them.

**On slide 5:** If you break the silence during the demo, you've lost the room. The sixty seconds of waiting IS the demonstration. Your stillness signals confidence. Any narration signals anxiety.

**On the close:** The last words are "The next presentation you give — it writes itself." Everything after that is a tax on the moment. Sit down immediately.

**On questions:** If someone asks a question mid-talk, answer it in one sentence and get back on script. The momentum is the product. Guard it.

**The one thing:** If the audience leaves remembering only one thing, it must be: *"Type a topic. Get a presentation."* Every word in this script exists to make that sentence feel inevitable.
